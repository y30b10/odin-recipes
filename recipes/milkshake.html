Milkshake

Description

A creamy and sweet strawberry milkshake made with real strawberries

Ingredients

  • 13 fresh strawberries
  • 4 scoops strawberry ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • 2 tablespoons whipped cream
  • 2 teaspoons chocolate sauce

Steps

  1. Blend strawberries, ice cream, milk, honey, and vanilla extract together in an electric blender. Blend in ice.
  2. Pour into glass, squirt on whipped cream, and drizzle chocolate sauce on top.
